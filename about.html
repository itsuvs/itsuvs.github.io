A Little Something About Me

Engineer turned marketer turned web developer. Building cool products is my latest gig and I am totally loving it. I am a self-taught coder, a mediocre hacker and a wannabe artist.

Forever a star-gazing wanderer, a misfit, always questioning, always seeking and constantly feeling burdened with a glorious purpose. I am the kind of person who looks for depth even in the shallowest places and who loves to root for the underdogs no matter what the circumstances be.
