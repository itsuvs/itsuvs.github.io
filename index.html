A Little Something About Me

I paid a small fortune for an MBA in Marketing, only to later fall for software engineering. An expensive plot twist, but honestly there are no regrets ;)

I started out in sales and advertising, but these days I build cool things on the internet—and I’m absolutely loving it. I currently work as a Senior Software Engineer at Cricbuzz, where I’ve helped build some of the most coolest and visited features on cricbuzz.com, including live cricket streaming, commentary, and scorecard.

At heart, I’m a star-gazing wanderer and a proud misfit—always questioning, always searching, and constantly feeling the weight of some grand and glorious purpose. I look for depth in the shallowest places and will cheer for the underdog every single time, no matter the odds.

* Full disclosure: I liberally employ Google's Gemini 3.1 Pro to write and proofread my blogs. It's like having a hyper-articulate brain extension that never sleeps and catches my midnight ideas and typos before they become public embarrassments.
