<br>

**mindgrep** is a local-first semantic document search CLI. It allows you to find your files by *meaning*, not keywords, entirely on your machine. It requires no cloud services and no API keys, ensuring your data remains private and secure.

<br>

### **Key Features:**

<br>

- **Semantic search**: Search across PDF, DOCX, XLSX, PPTX, TXT, and Markdown files.
- **Sub-second search**: Queries hit pre-built vectors, not raw files, for lightning-fast results.
- **Watch-based indexing**: The index updates automatically as files change in your watched folders.
- **100% local**: All embeddings and vectors never leave your machine.
- **Zero config**: Works out of the box with one simple command.

<br>

### **Future Roadmap:**

<br>

The next phase of development for mindgrep will focus on deeper document understanding and enhanced search capabilities. Future updates will include:
- **Document Summary**: Automatically generate concise summaries for your indexed documents to quickly grasp their contents.
- **Info Retrieval**: Advanced information extraction to directly answer questions based on your local documents.
- **Search Across Code Base**: Expanding semantic search capabilities to understand and index source code repositories, making it easier to find specific logic and implementations within your projects.
